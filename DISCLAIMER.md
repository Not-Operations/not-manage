# Disclaimer

`not-manage` is a local command-line tool. It is not a law firm compliance platform, and it does not replace legal review, privacy review, or firm policy.

## Important limits

- Redaction is best-effort only.
- The tool cannot guarantee that output is anonymous, non-privileged, or safe to share with third parties.
- The tool cannot determine whether a user's downstream AI or vendor workflow is allowed under firm policy or client obligations.

## Appropriate positioning

- Safer local handling: yes.
- Blanket compliance guarantee: no.
- Substitute for lawyer judgment or firm approval: no.

## Practical rule

If the data would create a problem when pasted into an external system, reviewed by a third party, or attached to a ticket, treat the output as sensitive and review it manually before sharing.
